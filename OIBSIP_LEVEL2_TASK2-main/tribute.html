Casey Neistat

YouTuber | Filmmaker | Entrepreneur

Casey Neistat is an American YouTube personality, filmmaker, and entrepreneur. He gained popularity for his vlogs, which chronicled his daily life and experiences. Neistat has won numerous awards for his short films, including a Cannes Lion and a Streamy Award. He also co- founded the multimedia company Beme and sold it to CNN in 2016. Neistat continues to be a major figure in the YouTube community and a recognized influencer in the tech and media industries.

The Sunken Cruise Ship

"The Sunken Cruise Ship" is a short film by Casey Neistat that documents his trip to the sunken Italian cruise ship Costa Concordia. Neistat and his team used a drone to capture footage of the wreckage and explore the abandoned ship. The film provides a haunting look at the aftermath of the disaster and has over 6 million views on YouTube.

Do What You Can't

"Do What You Can't" is a motivational video by Casey Neistat that encourages viewers to defy expectations and pursue their dreams, no matter the obstacles. The video has over 14 million views on YouTube and has inspired countless people around the world.
